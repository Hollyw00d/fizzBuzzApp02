Refactored Fizz Buzz App Using jQuery by Matt Jennings

Enter a number that is 1 - 100, then press Enter. Then you will see one of 4 messages listed below per the rules of the Fizz Buzz division game:

  • If the number you entered was divisible by 3, after you will see a Fizz! Your number is divisible by 3 message.
  • If the number you entered was divisible by 5, after you will see a Buzz! Your number is divisible by 5 message.
  • If the number you entered was divisible by 3 and 5, after you will see a Fizz Buzz! Your number is divisible by 3 and 5 message.
  • If you accidentally didn't follow the instructions listed above, you will see a Sorry, please try again message.
