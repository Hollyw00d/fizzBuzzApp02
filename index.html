Refactored Fizz Buzz App Using jQuery by Matt Jennings

Enter an integer that is 1 - 100, then press Enter. Then, per the rules of the Fizz Buzz division game numbers will be printed out, including the number you entered, with a few exceptions:

  • Numbers divisible by 3 will be replaced with Fizz.
  • Numbers divisible by 5 will be replaced with Buzz.
  • Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 will be replaced with Fizz Buzz.
